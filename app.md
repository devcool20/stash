💎 The Stash Philosophy: "Visual Serenity"Stash is not a productivity tool; it is a digital declutter utility.The Problem: Your camera roll is a messy "junk drawer" of ideas you’ll never find again.The Solution: Stash acts as a high-end visual inbox. You throw things in; AI (locally) makes them searchable; you find them months later in seconds.The Vibe: Dark, expensive, quiet, and incredibly fast.📱 The 4-Screen Uniformity BlueprintScreen 1: The Infinite Grid (Home)This is your "Digital Yard." It’s a 2-column masonry stream of everything you've saved.The Aesthetic: Deep black background (#000000). Cards are dark frosted glass (rgba(255,255,255,0.04)) with a subtle 1px border.The "Uniformity" Fix: The bottom of the screen has a smooth black gradient that "swallows" the cards as they scroll down, blending perfectly into the bottom navigation bar.Key Feature: A Floating Action Button (FAB) integrated into the bottom bar that says "Stash" — this is your one-tap entry point for new content.Screen 2: The Deep Inspect (Focus)When you tap a card in the grid, it expands into this "Focus View."The Aesthetic: The background blurs out entirely (backdrop-filter: blur(25px)). The image or link preview stays centered.The Detail: Under the image, a sleek glass panel displays the Extracted Text (OCR). Because the AI runs locally, you can tap any word in the image to copy it instantly.Uniformity: The "Close" button is a simple, thin X at the top right—no heavy buttons, just clean lines.Screen 3: The Library (Smart Filters)This is where the AI groups your mess into logic.The Aesthetic: The top 30% of the screen features large, horizontal-scrolling "Lenses" (Shopping, Design, Recipes, Travel).Uniformity: These lenses use the same corner radius (24px) as the cards in Screen 1. When you select "Shopping," the grid below updates instantly, but the layout doesn't move a millimeter—maintaining visual muscle memory.Key Feature: Each lens has a tiny "count" number (e.g., Recipes: 14) in a muted grey font.Screen 4: The Vault (Profile & Privacy)The settings screen that feels like a luxury car dashboard.The Aesthetic: Instead of white list rows, we use inset glass rows.The Info: This screen tells the user: "Your data is your own." It features a toggle for Local-Only Sync and a "Storage Used" meter that shows exactly how many megabytes your "Second Brain" is taking up on your device.Uniformity: The bottom navigation bar is identical to Screen 1 and 3—dark glass, high-contrast white icons.🛠️ Design Tokens (For Your Developer/AI Studio)ElementValueLogicCanvas#000000 (Pure Black)Saves battery on OLED and looks premium.Glass Panelsrgba(255, 255, 255, 0.04)Provides depth without being "heavy."Blur Radius20px to 30pxCreates the "smoked glass" luxury feel.Primary Text#FFFFFF (Pure White)For headers and titles.Muted Text#8A8A93 (Light Grey)For dates, tags, and descriptions.Corner Radius20pxConsistent across all cards, buttons, and modals.📢 The "What We Do" (App Intro/Onboarding)When the user first opens the app, they should see 3 simple, non-cluttered slides:Capture: "Screenshot anything.
